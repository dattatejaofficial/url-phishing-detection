How PhishGuard Works

PhishGuard protects you by scanning websites in real time as you browse. It uses machine-learning models trained on known phishing and legitimate URLs to identify suspicious patterns in links. When a risky website is detected, PhishGuard warns you before the page loads. Websites you mark as trusted are skipped to reduce false alerts, and your browsing data remains private at all times.
